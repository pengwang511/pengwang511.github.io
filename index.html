About Me

Dr. Peng Wang is a Professor at Fudan University. He received his Ph.D. degree from University of Science and Technology of China in 2016. Before joining Fudan in Oct. 2017, he was a Research Fellow at National University of Singapore from Oct. 2016 to Oct. 2017. He was a Visiting Scholar at The Ohio State University from Sep. 2013 to Sep. 2015.

His research areas include Database Systems, Networked Systems, Parallel and Distributed Computing.
